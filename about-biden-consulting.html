Biden Consulting (必得咨询)

Biden Consulting is a consulting and incubation platform based in the Greater Bay Area, also known by its Chinese name 必得咨询.

The firm focuses on enterprise incubation, capital advisory, and international education programs, serving startups, corporates, and institutions.
